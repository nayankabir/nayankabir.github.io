Animation and Film Producer: Job Description

GreenLight Canada is currently hiring highly motivated individuals who are looking to break into the animation industry. What sets our company apart from others is the flexibility in the tools that are used for development. This approach helps our employees work with tools they feel comfortable with while allowing more learning opportunities within the company for both paid and open source software. We are an exciting start-up company offering employees a unique opportunity to work with minimal supervision as well as completely remote positions.

GreenLight Canada is a start-up company in affiliation with the University of Toronto.

License Number: 270260110

As an Animation and Film Producer, you will be required to create videos that can be easily manipulated by the client, and have mechanisms in place for voiceovers to be implemented either prior to or following the animation development process. This position is completely remote and would require you to attend meetings via Skype or equivalent. You will be working closely with a team lead based out of Toronto, Canada.

Requirements for this role includes:

  • Able to demonstrate of a broad range of 2D animation skills; this may include a live demo of previous projects as part of the interview process
  • Ability to add visual effects is a plus
  • Voiceover capabilities such as lip syncing and mouth rigging is an asset
  • Access to a cell-phone camera that can record at 1080p for at least 30mins at a time
  • Access to a constant internet connection is preferred, with the ability to upload large amounts of data
In order to apply for this role, please submit your resumé to the Team Lead for this project: nayan.kabir@utoronto.ca
