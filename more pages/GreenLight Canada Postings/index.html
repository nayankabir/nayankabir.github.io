About The Company

GreenLight Canada is a digital media company in affiliation with the University of Toronto. For more information on our entrepreneurship with UofT, please visit: http://entrepreneurs.utoronto.ca/

We are currently hiring highly motivated individuals who are looking to break into the animation and film industry. What sets our company apart from others is the flexibility in the tools that are used for development. This approach helps our employees work with tools they feel comfortable with while allowing more learning opportunities within the company for both paid and open source software. We are an exciting start-up company offering employees a unique opportunity to work with minimal supervision while being able to work remotely from virtually anywhere in the world.

Digital Media Producer

Job Description & Requirements

As an Digital Media Producer, you will be required to create animations/videos that can be easily manipulated by the client, and have mechanisms in place for voiceovers to be implemented either prior to or following the animation development process. You will also be required to record public footages and produce waiver forms if necessary. This position is completely remote and would require you to attend meetings via Skype or equivalent. You will be working closely with a team lead based out of Toronto, Canada.

Requirements for this role includes:

  • Demonstrated abilities of 2D animation skills at least at a beginner level with tools such as ToonBoom, Pencil2D, etc.
  • Experience with any video editing software
  • Ability to add visual effects is a plus
  • Voiceover capabilities such as lip syncing and mouth rigging is an asset
  • Access to a camera or cell phone that can record 1080p video for at least 30mins
  • Access to a consistent internet connection is preferred, with the ability to upload large amounts of data
  • Communication skills are key; you must be able to either present or document your findings
